Key Projects

CR25 Rear Wing Design

Increased the efficiency of the rear wing by 11% and decreased the weight by almost 1.5 lbs. This was done by doing iterative design using CFD and SolidWorks to help reduced the pressure gradient and therefore reduce the vortices coming off the endplate. This helped reduce the induced drag and therefore increased efficiency.
